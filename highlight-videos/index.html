Steps Required

Collect your clips

Download or timestamp specific clips and provide access to them. Archives of public livestreams and self recorded content is typically highest quality, screen recording reduces quality.

Pick a song

Decide on a song to use. Most highlight videos have pump up music which is normally electronic, DJ, etc.

Can’t find a song? Fear not! Suggestions are always provided

Gather your stats

Every video comes with a title card that showcases the athlete’s statistics, achievements, and current information. Make sure that this is handy and up to date as it is crucial to the video & the recruitment process.

WATCH AN EXAMPLE

Make sure you stand out

As well as zooming and cropping, callout boxes and targets can be added to make sure that the athlete is always the center of attention.
